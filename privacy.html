Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2026

Overview

Vertical Habits ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Your name (optional, provided during onboarding)
  • Habit tracking data (checkmarks, dates)
  • Session logs (climbing grades, notes)
  • Training preferences (discipline, goals)
  • Premium subscription status
  • Custom habits you create

All of this data remains on your device. We cannot access it, view it, or use it in any way.

Payment Processing

Payments are processed securely through:

  • iOS: Apple App Store (subject to Apple's privacy policy)
  • Android: Google Play Store (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Web: Stripe (subject to Stripe's privacy policy)

We do not store, process, or have access to your payment information, credit card numbers, or billing details.

Analytics

We do not currently use any analytics or tracking services. Your usage of the app is completely private.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties because we don't collect any data to begin with.

Data Export & Deletion

Export: You can export all your data at any time from the Profile tab in the app. This creates a JSON file containing all your habits, sessions, and preferences.

Delete: You can delete all your data by:

  • Clearing browser data/cache
  • Uninstalling the app
  • Using the "Reset All Data" option in the app (if available)

Offline Functionality

Vertical Habits works entirely offline. The app uses a service worker to cache files for offline access, but this does not involve sending data to servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we don't collect data, there are no age restrictions on app usage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored on your device:

  • You have full control over your data
  • You can export it anytime
  • You can delete it anytime
  • No third parties have access to it

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at:

support@verticalhabits.com

Summary

Privacy-First Design: Vertical Habits is designed with privacy as a core principle. All your data stays on your device. We cannot access it, and neither can anyone else. You have complete control.
